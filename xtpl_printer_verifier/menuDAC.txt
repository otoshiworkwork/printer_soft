
Buttons: 
a - Signal ON
s - Signal OFF
d - send pulse [WORK IN PROGRESS]
h/j - decrease/increase pulse voltage by 500
v/b - decrease/increase pulse time by 10ns
k/l - decrease/increase Vac by 500
n/m - decrease/increase Vdc by 100
q - auto-off after 60 seconds
r - reverse DC direction
o - leave Signal Control Menu...
